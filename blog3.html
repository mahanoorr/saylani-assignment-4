What is Digital Finance?

Digital Finance is the innovative idea of bringing process developments into the accounting and finance fields. In simple words idea that individuals and companies can have access to payments, savings, and credit products without ever stepping into a bank branch.



There are five pillars of digital finance. The Five Pillars of Digital Finance:

AUTOMATION:

the process by which a procedure is performed with the least human support.

VISUALIZATION:

visualization is a process of creating images diagrams and animation for communication.

ETL/SQL:

ETL is short for extract, transform, load, three database functions that are merged into one tool to pull data out of one database and place it into another database using SQL (Standard Query Language).

CLOUD & BLOCKCHAIN:

It is an online ledger technology that provides information to a company and helps them record their transactions easily.

MACHINE LEARNING & AI:

Machine learning is a subset of AI which allows a machine to automatically receive from past data without programming explicitly. It enables a machine to imitate human behavior.

Benefits of Digital Finance:


  • Accessibility.
  • Efficient to use.
  • Save lots of time and resources no need to wait in queues for fund transfer.
  • The efficiency of decision making.
  • Reliability in changing a transaction.
  • Eco friendly.
  • Increases the influence through omnichannel digital marketing.
