Advantages & Disadvantages of Robots | CS KNOCKS

The robot:


Robot originates from the Czech word “robota” which means “forced work or labor.” The study about robots is called Robotics. Robots are made to perform all operations that a human can perform so that’s why they are also called humanoid robots. It carries a multifarious web of operations. Robots are made to replace humans because they are the substitutes of humans. They do all the work a human can do and perform all those actions and perform all those operations. They are also employed in some jobs.



Advantages of using robots:

  • Robots are obey.
  • They are loyal to their work.
  • They don’t say to their boss.
  • They can’t harm human.
  • They protect themselves also.
  • They increase productivity.
  • They also increase profit margin.
  • They don’t need break.

Disadvantages of robots over humans:

  • Robots have no sense of feelings or conscience.
  • They are not intelligent as humans.
  • Robots only perform those operations fed on them using chip.
  • They require a manual labor with them.
  • Their cost is so high.
