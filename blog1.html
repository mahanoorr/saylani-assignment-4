Introduction:

The term IoT stands for the Internet of Things. The term internet of things is devised by Kevin Ashton in 1999. Internet of things (IoT) means the things connected to the internet for doing automation and monitoring a thing from outside. in short, The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical gadgets or "things" embedded with electronics, software, sensors, which empowers these objects to gather and transfer data.

How IoT work?

these devices formerly called "smart" devices because they follow the instructions given by the human on their own. An IoT system consists of sensors that interact with the system through some connection. When instruction goes into the system, after processes than it elect to act, such as sending alerts, etc without the requirement of the human being. IoT is simply the internet of everything.

Future of IoT:

From 2020 to 2030, IoT devices will grow from 75 billion to more than 100 billion, and the improvement from 4G to 5G in terms to grow IOT is most important. 5g is just like fuel for IoT.
