5G network technology | CS KNOCKS

Introduction:

In 5G the G stands for a generation so 5G technology is the 5th generation wireless network technology.No company or person owns the 5g networking but some companies are contributing to bringing 5G to life. It connects people, machines and devices virtually together.5G networks use a type of encoding called OFDM, which is similar to the encoding that 4G LTE uses. The air interface is designed for much lower latency and greater flexibility than LTE.

How does it work?

it’s assigned a higher radiofrequency. For instance, 4G occupied the frequency bands up to 20 MHz. In the case of 5G, it will likely lie on the frequency band up to 6GHz. The reason new wireless technologies own higher frequencies is that they typically aren’t in use and move information at a much faster speed.

Benefits of 5g technology:

  • Faster in speed.
  • More flowing streaming of online content
  • the greater capacity of remote execution
  • Higher-quality voice and video calls
  • More reliable mobile connections
  • Greater number of connected IOT devices
  • More effective

5G enabling technologies:

  • Massive MIMO
  • Green communications
  • Visible-light communication
  • Cloud computing
  • Millimeter-wave communication.
