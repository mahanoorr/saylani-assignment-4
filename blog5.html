INTRODUCTION OF COMPUTER:

The word computer is coined from a Latin word computare which means” to compute” and ”to calculate”. A computer is an electronic device used to store data. It is the device which globalizes the whole world in its hands. The process from abacus to laptop Is very difficult now discuss some importance of it.

History of computer:

  • 1946-1959 it is the era in which vacuum tubes are developed.
  • 1959-1965 it is the era in which transistors are developed.
  • 1965-1971 it is the era in which Integrated circuits are developed.
  • 1971-1980 it is the era in which VLSI microprocessors are developed.
  • 1980-onwards it is the era in which ULSI microprocessor is developed
  • Then it became personal computers, laptops and palmtops which is the evaluation of the computer.
The major names of scientists in the development of computer are:

  • Charles Babbage (The Father of Computing)

  • Lady Ada Augusta(first computer programmer)

  • Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz

  • Blaise Pascal

What is computer science?

Computer science is an art which is used to coordinate the user with the computer through programming and algorithms.
It includes both hardware and software knowledge, the hardware area covers the use of engineering drawings and drafting while software area covers programming, Algorithms and operating systems, etc.
